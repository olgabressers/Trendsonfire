5. Salary & labour market data

TrendsOnFire publishes salary indices, layoff trackers, and other labour market analyses. The following limitations apply to this data:

  • Salary figures represent market observations derived from publicly available sources (job postings, industry surveys, published reports) and are not the result of formal compensation surveys
  • Individual compensation depends on many factors not captured in aggregate data, including company size, location, scope, performance, negotiation, and benefits structure
  • Layoff data is sourced from public announcements, news reports, and industry databases — completeness cannot be guaranteed
  • All figures are indicative and may be outdated by the time of reading

Do not use salary or headcount data on this site as the sole basis for employment decisions, compensation negotiations, or HR planning without verifying figures through dedicated compensation surveys and legal counsel.
