Key Omni-Channel Trends — 2026
🏪 BOPIS
Buy Online, Pick Up In Store is no longer differentiating — it's table stakes
BOPIS adoption has crossed the tipping point. Retailers without it are losing 12–18% of transactions to competitors. The new battleground is speed: same-hour pickup is the next frontier.
🎁 Loyalty
Unified loyalty is the single most impactful omni-channel investment
Retailers with unified loyalty programmes across channels report 2.4× higher repeat purchase rates. Yet the majority still run separate online/offline points systems that confuse and frustrate customers.
↩️ Returns
Cross-channel returns are the hidden loyalty driver most brands are failing
Allowing online purchases to be returned in-store increases in-store basket size by 22% on average — customers who come in to return often buy again. Many retailers still block this flow.
📦 Fulfilment
Ship-from-store is becoming a competitive advantage, not a fallback
Leading retailers are using stores as micro-fulfilment hubs to cut delivery times by 40–60%. This requires real-time inventory systems — the retailers who invested in visibility are now reaping the fulfilment dividend.
🧑 Customer Data
The single customer view is still a goal, not a reality, for most retailers
Only 31% of mid-to-large retailers have achieved a true unified customer profile across channels. CDPs are being deployed at pace, but data quality and organisational silos remain the blockers.
