Methodology & Data Sources

Salary ranges are aggregated from publicly available compensation platforms:

  • Glassdoor — self-reported compensation data (sample sizes vary by country and role)
  • PayScale — salary benchmarks with percentile breakdowns
  • Robert Half Salary Guide 2026 — covers NL, DE, UK, FR, BE, CH, LU
  • Michael Page Salary Guide 2026 — covers NL, BE, CH and other European markets

Figures represent total cash compensation (base + typical annual bonus) for director/VP-level roles with 10–20 years experience. Local currency figures converted to EUR at 2025 exchange rates (UK in GBP, Switzerland in CHF). Only markets with sufficient Glassdoor/PayScale sample sizes are included. Actual compensation varies by company size, sector, equity component, and individual profile.
