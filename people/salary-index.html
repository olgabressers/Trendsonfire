Methodology

Every figure on this page is taken from a named, publicly accessible compensation source — PayScale Netherlands, Glassdoor Netherlands, and Nationale Beroepengids — and linked inline to the exact role URL. All figures are gross (bruto) annual or monthly, in EUR, for the Netherlands.

Sources used:

  • PayScale Netherlands — self-reported compensation with percentile breakdowns
  • Glassdoor Netherlands — user-submitted salaries with sample size disclosed per role
  • Nationale Beroepengids — Dutch labour-market reference platform (source pages in Dutch)

What is not on this page: Robert Half NL 2026 and Michael Page NL 2026 salary guides are gated (PDF download behind a form), so their figures are not cited here — a registered reader can download them directly for cross-reference. Country coverage beyond the Netherlands, and additional roles (including E-Commerce Director), require public sample sizes that are not yet large enough to publish defensible benchmarks. These will be added when solid public sources are located.

Caveats that apply to every figure: actual compensation varies by company size, sector, equity/LTIP component, and individual profile. Sample sizes per role are small in several cases — treat the figures as reference ranges, not hiring quotes.
