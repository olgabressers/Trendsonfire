Methodology & Data Sources

Assessments in this index are based on publicly available information — including company annual reports, LinkedIn organisational data, published case studies, press releases, and professional industry knowledge. Each criterion reflects observable, documented behaviour rather than self-reported claims.

This is a living index, updated as new information becomes available. Scores reflect the state of publicly available evidence at time of publication and may not capture internal programmes not visible externally. Organisations are assessed at the CEE regional level, not individual country subsidiaries.

Rating scale: Advanced = 5–6 criteria met · Developing = 3–4 criteria met · Basic = 0–2 criteria met. Assessments are made independently and organisations have not been contacted for verification. See below to submit a correction.
