Geography

Where they live

  • Torrevieja — largest concentration; established Russian quarter with Russian shops, pharmacies and restaurants
  • Guardamar del Segura — many permanent Russian residents
  • Orihuela Costa — mixed expat, large Russian presence
  • Alicante city — urban professionals, younger generation
  • Benidorm, Calpe, Altea — northern Costa Blanca

Torrevieja is the natural hub for a Russian-language event. Accessible to all Costa Blanca without requiring a long drive.

Target participant

Who is the audience

  • Age 35–60, homeowners or long-term residents
  • Women over-represented in personal development
  • Retirees and property owners with time and disposable income
  • Entrepreneurs and self-employed (flexible schedule)
  • Post-2022 Ukrainian arrivals now stabilised
  • Prior interest in meditation, NLP, coaching, holistic practices
Demand signal

Why demand exists

  • Torrevieja community is established — people seek growth, not just survival
  • Silva Method is internationally recognised — not an "alternative" niche
  • No Russian-language personal development training offered locally in Costa Blanca
  • October timing: 22–26°C, sunny, post-summer energy, community active
  • Ирина has European reputation — her name travels ahead of her

Venue Options

Three options for the 3-day training space. Requirements: space for 10–20 participants seated, suitable for meditation (quiet, controllable light), accessible in or near Torrevieja.

Most Affordable

Alena's Livingroom

€300
Total venue cost · 3 days

Private home space — intimate atmosphere, flexible layout, no venue logistics to manage. Lowest cost option. Suitable for smaller groups. Home setting creates a warm, personal experience well-suited for meditation training.

  • Up to 10–15 participants
  • Warm, low-formality atmosphere
  • Kitchen access for coffee and snacks
  • No external AV needed for small groups
Mid Option

SPACE

€450
Total venue cost · 3 days

Dedicated studio or co-working space suitable for group training. More structured than a private home — better acoustics and layout for 15–20 participants. Professional feel without high hotel pricing.

  • Basic AV available

Best Dates for 2026 — Friday–Saturday–Sunday Format

3-day module: Friday afternoon start → Sunday evening close. Attendees need to arrange Friday off, or target self-employed, retirees, and people with flexible schedules. October in Torrevieja: 22–26°C, sunny, low humidity — outstanding conditions for a multi-day personal development programme.

OptionDatesDaysAssessmentNotes
Option 1 — Preferred 16–17–18 October 2026 Fri · Sat · Sun ✓✓ Recommended Best window. 6+ weeks before year-end. Strong autumn energy. Audience fully settled after summer. 8-week promotion runway from early bird 1 Sep deadline.
Option 2 — Backup 23–24–25 October 2026 Fri · Sat · Sun ✓ Good One week later. Still viable — no Spanish bank holidays on these days. Slightly closer to November. Use if trainer's schedule conflicts with Option 1.

Note: 12 October is Spain's Fiesta Nacional (Monday) — does not conflict with either Fri–Sun option. No Valencian regional bank holidays fall on 16–18 or 23–25 October.
