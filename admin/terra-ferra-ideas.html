Ideas This Raises for Terra Ferra
01
Full Attribution Transparency — Build It In from Day One
ShopMy is valued at $1.5B precisely because it shows brands exactly which ambassador drove each sale. LTK withholds this and is now struggling to pivot. Every TF designer should see in real time which ambassador and which superfan drove which order. This is the structural differentiator that makes designers stay and pay.
02
ShopMy "Circles" → TF Co-Creation Feed
ShopMy's consumer app shows curated collections from creators the user follows. TF's version: curated drops by designers + the superfan community that co-created them — the design story as content, not just a product listing. ShopMy proves this consumer layer is where the next billion in GMV comes from. Plan it from the start, not as Phase 2.
03
Ablo's AI Design Tool for TF Designers
TF targets designers at ~$1M revenue, 5K–50K followers — exactly the stage where they have creative vision but limited technical design capability. Ablo Studio (or similar API) could be integrated as TF's AI design tool: sketch-to-digital, on-demand prototyping. Removes the friction between creative idea and shareable pre-order prototype.
04
Financial Royalties for Co-Creators, Not Just Credit
Ablo auto-distributes royalties when a design is remixed. TF's co-creation gives named credit — this should also be financial. When a superfan's colour or silhouette input is used in the final collection, an automatic royalty (cash or platform credit) flows to them. Turns a loyalty mechanic into a genuine economic stake.
05
Premium Curation Over Volume
ShopMy wins with 1,200 premium brands vs LTK's 5,000 mass-market brands. TF should resist onboarding any designer who applies. A curated, high-trust designer roster protects consumer experience and brand perception. Selective = premium. This is also TF's only realistic competitive angle against LTK's scale advantage.
06
The Consumer App Is the Flywheel, Not Phase 2
ShopMy built B2B tools first, then launched "Circles" consumer app in July 2025 and immediately validated three-sided model economics. TF's consumer taste profile, wishlist, and co-creation feed should be designed from day one. The designers need the consumer-side data to justify staying on TF vs going direct to Instagram.
