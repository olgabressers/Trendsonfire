About the Silva Method

What is the Silva Method?

Developed by José Silva in the 1960s, the Silva Method is a self-help and personal development program based on training the mind to function at the alpha brainwave level — a relaxed, meditative state in which learning, creativity, and intuition are said to be enhanced. It is one of the longest-running personal development programmes in the world, with millions of graduates in 110+ countries.

The programme is structured in two modules, each delivered over a 2-day live session. Anastasia Detzel is a certified Silva Method trainer conducting sessions in Russian — based in Estonia and one of very few certified Russian-language trainers in Europe.

Module 1 — Silva Life System (2 days)

Foundation: entering the alpha state, the glass of water technique, mental screen visualisation, stress and sleep management, goal-setting through mental rehearsal. Suitable for complete beginners.

Module 2 — Silva Intuition System (2 days)

Advanced: developing intuition, psychometry, case working, remote sensing. Builds directly on Module 1 skills. Recommended to complete both in sequence.
