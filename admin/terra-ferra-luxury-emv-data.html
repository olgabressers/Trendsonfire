Three Findings — Hard Luxury, Affordable Luxury, Social Commerce

01
Hard luxury uses influencers for brand building — not direct sales conversion.
Dior generated $84M EMV from a single Paris fashion week. Cartier's ambassador Jisoo drove 45% of annual brand EMV from 12 posts. None of it converts on-platform by design. The mechanics are powerful; the transaction link is deliberately absent.

Lefty Womenswear AW25 · WeArisma / WWD, Feb 2025

02
Affordable luxury sells through social — and the data shows it clearly.
Coach added 4.6 million new North American customers in FY25, approximately 70% Gen Z or Millennial, driven by creator and social strategy. Burberry's London fashion week EMV grew 83% year-on-year. These brands use creators to acquire customers, not just amplify the brand.

Tapestry FY25 earnings release · Lefty AW25 report

03
Social commerce converts — when the infrastructure exists to support it.
TikTok Shop reached $64B GMV globally in 2025, 60% creator-driven. Pre-owned luxury resellers generated £2M on TikTok Shop (UK) and $30,000+ in a single day (US). The consumer behaviour is proven. The gap is who has access to the infrastructure.

Momentum Works, Feb 2026 · Influencer Marketing Hub, Jun 2025 · The Keyword, Dec 2025

Methodology — MIV vs EMV

MIV (Media Impact Value) is Launchmetrics' proprietary metric. It factors in content quality, source authority, and industry relevance — not engagement alone. It is the industry standard for luxury brands and fashion weeks.

EMV (Earned Media Value) is used by Lefty and WeArisma. It is primarily engagement-based with platform multipliers applied. MIV and EMV figures for the same event will differ and are not directly comparable across sources.

Revenue attribution: no luxury house — Richemont, LVMH, Kering, Hermès, Chanel — publishes influencer-to-sales conversion data. MIV and EMV measure media impact generated, not transactions completed. Richemont's FY24 total "communication expenses" were €2.0B (9.7% of sales), with no influencer line item disclosed. (Richemont FY24 results, richemont.com)
