Trend Intelligence

Trending
Podcast listenership in the Netherlands crosses 5 million regular listeners
Over 40% of Dutch internet users now listen to podcasts monthly. Spotify dominates with ~72% of NL podcast consumption, but YouTube audio is growing fast as a second platform.
Trending
Podcast advertising revenue surpasses $4 billion globally in 2025
Host-read ads deliver the highest trust scores of any digital ad format. CPM rates for niche B2B shows outperform most display and social formats.
Trending
Video podcasts are redefining the format — YouTube becomes a primary discovery channel
Over 60% of new podcast listeners discover shows via YouTube. Recording video alongside audio has become the default for professional podcasters targeting broad reach.
Trending
Niche B2B podcasts are outperforming general business shows on engagement
Industry-specific shows (retail tech, supply chain, pharma, fintech) average 3x higher listener engagement than general business formats. Thought leadership is shifting from LinkedIn posts to podcast conversations.
Trending
Podcasts are becoming a measurable DTC sales driver, not just an awareness channel
Promo codes, landing pages, and QR-code tracking have made podcast ROI measurable. DTC brands in NL and Belgium are allocating 15-20% of media budgets to podcast sponsorships.
Trending
AI-generated podcast summaries and clips are driving discovery at scale
Platforms now auto-generate highlight clips, transcriptions, and SEO-optimised episode summaries. A single 45-minute episode can produce 20+ social media assets with minimal effort.
