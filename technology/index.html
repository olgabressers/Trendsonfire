Key Technology Trends — 2026
🤖 AI in Retail
AI is separating the top quartile from the rest — the gap is widening fast
Retailers using AI for demand forecasting, personalisation, and operations are seeing 2–4× better outcomes. The laggards aren't just behind — they're falling further behind every quarter.
⚙️ Operating Models
The AI-first operating model requires role redesign, not just tool deployment
Organisations adding AI as a bolt-on to existing structures are not seeing the gains. The leaders are redesigning workflows, decision rights, and team structures around AI capabilities.
📊 Data Strategy
First-party data is now a strategic asset — most organisations are still treating it as IT
With third-party signals declining, retailers and commerce businesses that have invested in zero-party and first-party data strategies are building durable competitive advantages.
🔗 Integration
Legacy system integration is the hidden blocker of digital transformation
85% of transformation programmes hit legacy integration walls. Organisations that invest in API-first architecture and composable commerce stacks are moving significantly faster.
🛡️ Digital Trust
Consumer trust in AI-driven experiences is the next differentiator
Shoppers increasingly notice and respond to AI-driven personalisation — positively when transparent, negatively when intrusive. Digital trust is becoming a brand metric.
