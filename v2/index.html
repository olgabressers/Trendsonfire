About this Monitor

This tracker assesses how well Netherlands-based retailers surface real-time inventory signals to shoppers across digital storefronts. Each retailer is audited against six criteria. N/A is applied where a criterion is structurally inapplicable (e.g. store inventory for online-only retailers). Data collected via manual audit of websites and apps, March 2026.

Assessment Criteria

  • Online Stock Status — Live in/out-of-stock on product page
  • Store-Level Inventory — Check availability by store location
  • Low Stock Alerts — "Nog maar X op voorraad" messaging
  • Back-in-Stock Notify — Sign up for restock alerts
  • Variant Availability — Per-size / per-colour stock shown
  • Delivery Date Accuracy — Estimated delivery tied to real inventory
