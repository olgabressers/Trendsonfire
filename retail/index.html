Key Retail & E-Commerce Trends — 2026
🔥 Inventory Visibility
Real-time stock transparency is now a conversion driver, not a nice-to-have
Retailers showing live stock levels, low-stock alerts, and variant availability see 15–25% higher add-to-cart rates. Yet 47% of NL fashion retailers still don't surface stock signals at variant level.
📦 Fulfilment
Delivery date accuracy has become a trust signal — not just a logistics metric
Shoppers treat promised delivery dates as a purchase commitment. Retailers who miss dates lose 34% of that customer's future spend. Only 53% of NL retailers currently show delivery dates tied to real inventory.
🏬 Store Integration
Store-level inventory visibility is the new baseline for omni-channel retail
Department stores and electronics retailers lead on store-level stock visibility. Fashion lags significantly — only 4 out of 13 NL fashion brands assessed allow shoppers to check store-level availability online.
🔔 Engagement
Back-in-stock notifications: the most under-used re-engagement tool in retail
Brands with back-in-stock alerts recover up to 22% of lost demand. Luxury brands are missing this almost entirely — only 2 of 5 NL luxury brands assessed offer this feature.
🛍️ Luxury Retail
Luxury's deliberate scarcity strategy conflicts with digital shopper expectations
Luxury brands resist low-stock urgency messaging to protect brand positioning. But digital-native luxury shoppers expect transparency. Tiffany & Co leads with 5/6 criteria — others risk losing younger affluent consumers.
⚡ Checkout Experience
One-click checkout is no longer a luxury — it's an expectation
Brands that haven't simplified payment flows are losing 20–30% of carts at checkout. BNPL, digital wallets, and one-click options are now baseline expectations for shoppers under 45.
🏪 BOPIS
Buy Online, Pick Up In Store is no longer differentiating — it's table stakes
BOPIS adoption has crossed the tipping point. Retailers without it are losing 12–18% of transactions to competitors. The new battleground is speed: same-hour pickup is the next frontier.
